Avong nen Romy

Located in the cool highlands of La Trinidad, Benguet, Avong nen Romy is a nature-based destination that offers a perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. Surrounded by pine trees and fresh mountain air, it provides a peaceful escape where visitors can unwind while enjoying exciting outdoor activities.

Originally developed as a private property, it has grown into a well-known eco-tourism site that promotes wellness, recreation, and community engagement. Its natural setting makes it an ideal place for families, friends, and groups looking to experience both calm and adrenaline in one location.

where people can recharge, bond, and create lasting memories.
